5 Steps To Prevent Coronavirus (COVID-19)

Author: Raj Ghosh Posted on: 1/4/2020

coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak spread all over the world, currently there is no cure to prevent coronavirus, but here are the 5 steps to prevent coronavirus from spreading.

What is coronavirus (COVID-19)?

Coronavirus (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus. The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing.

What are the steps to prevent coronavirus?

Here are the five important steps that you've to strictly follow, in order to prevent coronavirus affects you or others.

  • STEP 1: Wash Hands often.
  • STEP 2: Cough or sneeze into your elbow.
  • STEP 3: Don't touch your face with dirty hands
  • STEP 4: Try to keep safe distance (about 1-3 meters)
  • STEP 5: Stay home, until you need to fulfill your basic needs

How to maintain social distancing?

Maintaing social distancing will help us to fight and to prevent coronavirus to spread, here we're going to discuss about this topic on how to maintain social distancing.

To maintain social distancing first thing you can do is staying at home, if you've any urgency to go out of your home, then follow this steps to keep you safe outside.

  • STEP 1: Wear Mask, It will help you and others not to spread any kind of virus.
  • STEP 2: Wear full sleeve shirts, by wearing full sleeve shirts it gives you a extra protective layer to keep you safe from any kind of virus that might affect you or other.
  • STEP 3: Look for others, we suggest everyone to wear mask, if you found anyone who haven't wearing any mask please keep a safe distance and ask them to wear a mask.
  • STEP 4: In this crucial steps we recommend everyone not to shake hands.
  • STEP 5: Wash your hands, after you return home.

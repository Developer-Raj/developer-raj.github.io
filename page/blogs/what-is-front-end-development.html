What is Front-End Development

Author: Raj Ghosh Posted on: 2/4/2020

Front-End Development is the development of a website that web designer designs for it, in-short front-end development is the process of turning a web-design into a client-side website.

What are the requirements to become a Front-End Developer?

There are no requirements to become a front-end developer. The only thing you need to learn is HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript that is the basics to become a front-end developer.

Why do we need to become a Front-End Developer?

There are many reasons to become a front-end developer, the main reasons behind it are there is a lot of opportunities for a front-end developer to replenish its current skills, a junior front-end developer average annual salary is $50, 000 dollars that is equivalent to $4166 a monthly average salary. There is more position in this field of a front-end developer some of them are.

  • • Front-End Developer
  • • Front-End UI Developer
  • • Senior Front-End Developer
  • • Junior Front-End Developer
  • • HTML/CSS Developer
  • • Front-End SEO Expert

These are the few you can become you want to. There are a lot more positions in this field, I named a few of them, there is more you can do after you become a front-end developer you've lots of option to choose.

Job offer's for a Front-End Developer

Front-end developers had lots of job opportunities some of them are working remotely, working as a freelancer, working as a position, and so on.

What comes after a front-end developer?

After you become a front-end developer and want to move ahead there is an option to become a back-end developer. We'll discuss back-end development in an upcoming blog.

Conclusion

In this blog, we've discussed a topic on a front-end development and we learned that a front-end developer has lots of opportunities to replenish their skills and to become more effective in jobs, a front-end developer also has an option to work as a freelance, or to work remotely.
